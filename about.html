About Kairos Meal Preps

Kairos began as a simple weight loss challenge group at a local gym in Spokane, Washington.

The challenge aimed to inspire members to make lasting healthy changes. We chose the name Kairos, derived from the Greek word meaning “a significant moment in time” or “a moment of opportunity.” This name perfectly encapsulated our mission: this challenge was the pivotal moment we would look back on as the start of our healthier, transformed lives.

Kairos: A Moment of Opportunity became our rallying cry.

Realizing the True Obstacles: Joining a Gym Wasn’t Enough.

Despite everyone's initial enthusiasm and commitment, we faced common obstacles. The gym was only part of the equation. The real challenge lay in what we did outside the gym—our dietary habits and lifestyle choices. It was clear that without addressing these, achieving long-term results was nearly impossible.

Beyond the Gym – The Role of Nutrition.

To tackle the problem, we began providing tailored nutrition plans. These plans included full macro breakdowns, recipes, snacks, tips, and even strategies for enjoying the occasional alcoholic beverage without derailing progress.

While the phrase “Abs are made in the kitchen” holds true, implementing these changes was no small feat. We quickly realized that it wasn’t just about attending the gym regularly. We had to overhaul our eating habits, rethink grocery shopping, meticulously measure food, meal prep for the next day, and repeat this routine constantly. This became a daunting task for many participants.

The Breakthrough – Prepping for Success

We realized the major barrier to success was the complexity and time commitment of meal prep. People found it inconvenient and time-consuming to prepare, measure, and organize their meals. We saw an opportunity to make a significant impact by taking on this task for them.

We started meal prepping for those in our group, and the results were phenomenal. Participants saved hours every week, enjoyed consistent physical progress, and experienced a sense of relief and ease in managing their diet.

Growing Kairos

Fast forward to today, Kairos Meal Prep has grown far beyond its humble beginnings. What started as a small weight loss challenge group has evolved into a trusted meal prep service, helping individuals across Spokane and Coeur d'Alene save time and eat healthy with weekly meal deliveries.

We take the hassle out of healthy eating by cooking, measuring, and portioning each meal to perfection. Every meal is crafted fresh in our Spokane Valley kitchen, ensuring consistency in calories, macros, and quality.

Kairos is more than just meal delivery – we’re here to help you fuel your busy lifestyle with delicious, nutritious meals that make reaching your goals easier than ever. Join us and simplify your journey to healthier living!
